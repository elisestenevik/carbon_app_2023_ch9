Home

This app seeks to help individuals and cooperations to keep track of their carbon emissions from the transport sector. By knowing exctactly the size of your transport footprint, and which transport method you have the biggest potential to improve, we hope to incentivise our users to reduce their total emissions. We are currently working on providing the users with customized suggestions on ways to most efficently reduce your carbon emissions.

This app was originally developed during the cource FOR19 at NHH held by Mario Blazquez. The entire app exists because of the teaching and methods developed by him. Thank you Mario!

Mario Blazguez de Paz

Mario Blázquez de Paz was appointed postdoc researcher at the Department of Business and Management Science, NHH in 2020. Previously, he did another two postdocs, one at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology in Trondheim and another at the Research Institute of Industrial Economics (IFN) in Stockholm. He completed a Master in Economics at European University Institute and a PhD. in Economics at University of Bologna. His research focuses on the analysis of electricity markets in the presence of transmission constraints and the design of electricity auctions using theoretical models and experiments. Currently, he is working analysing the impact of Renewables Certificates of Origin on the competition in electricity markets.
